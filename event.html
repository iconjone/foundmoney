Happy Hour

Please join us for appetizers, beverages and an overview of the solution that has funded more than $700 Million of benefits in Great Britain and is now being introduced to the US. “Point of Need Care Funding” is helpful to those who did not buy or cannot qualify for long term care insurance but need extended care. It helps families reduce stress by ensuring that their loved one will receive guaranteed, sustainable income for as long as they live, even after a diagnosis of chronic illness. Mixing and mingling starts at 4:30 pm, and at 5:15 pm we’ll have an interview and Q&A with Barry J. Fisher, consultant for US Point of Need Care Funding and columnist for Broker World Magazine.

HEALTHCARE

The cost of healthcare has been rising faster than the consumer price index for many years. No matter how “rich” the plan, or how generous the employer share of costs, people find reasons to complain. Common complaints include the cost of premiums, deductibles, coinsurance, emergency room visits, x-rays, anesthesiologists, specialists, out of network providers, prescription drugs, the need for precertification, and more. Sometimes options exist to lessen the pain (no pun intended). Often a little knowledge and guidance can make things more tolerable. Education, consultation, and knowing how the plan works can help a lot. We do that. And we also search for ways to make things better by asking the right questions, not only of the participant but also the carrier or claims administrator.

TAX REDUCTION

Every year many people pay more taxes than necessary, especially payroll taxes.
This happens due to lack of knowing how to legally reduce tax liability in compliance with the IRS Code. We have developed unique tools to analyze immediate tax saving opportunities to employees in a group session, followed by a one-on-one consultation and analysis that includes a personal, customized paycheck illustration with “Before” and “After” Scenarios. Best of all we accomplish this by communicating in down-to-earth, everyday layman’s language backed up with factual data and accurate calculations tailored to the individual employees wishes.

CASH FLOW

In 2018 nearly half of all Americans would be hard pressed to come up with $400 to cover an unexpected emergency. A lot of us don’t know where our money goes (or went) and consequently find ourselves financially strapped for cash. To help with this situation, we’ve identified relatively painless, small, incremental actions that allow people keep more of our hard-earned money. As a result, they become more consciously aware of the true cost of our decisions and their cumulative value over a year, a decade or a lifetime.

MANAGING RISK

Most people won’t go sky-diving, mountain climbing, or jumping off a cliff. However, we all face elements of risk in our lives. Besides obvious physical risks of daily life, there are risks of the unexpected, unplanned emergencies, financial risks, liability as a driver or property owner, health risks, economic conditions, loss of a job, disability, injury, extended care for ourselves or a loved one, and loss of life. Beyond common sense most people try to reduce, minimize or shift away a portion of those risks to insurers. But, do they have the right coverages in the right amounts? Are they paying too much? We’ll be glad to review, make recommendations, and research options where appropriate, all without a sales pitch.

RETIREMENT PLANNING

One of the biggest challenges facing people today is outliving their money or being able to sustain their lifestyle after retiring. Social Security will not provide enough income to support even a modest lifestyle. Old fashioned pension plans (that pay a defined benefit based on age at retirement and years of service) have largely been eliminated by corporate America. Nowadays, if available, employees may save into an employer sponsored 401(k) plan, maybe with a matching contribution. However, some 27% of workers cannot afford to save, 18% are too confused to make a decision, another 18% are not eligible, and at least 16% won’t sign up.
Furthermore, the median retirement savings account today contains a meager $15,000. At this pace our population is on track for a retirement crisis. Through solid, effective communications in workshops and one-on-one consultations we can help at least a portion of the population to get on track.

SURVEYS

FOCUS GROUPS

One of our most effective ways to elicit and document real, open, honest feedback from employees is a focus group conducted by an objective, unbiased, capable moderator who is naturally inquisitive, questions for clarification, and facilitates the group in an empathetic and non-judgmental manner. These sessions can be very enlightening, fun, and often revealing of true feelings and perceptions that may otherwise not be expressed.

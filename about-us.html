Steve Gonzales

Founder And Ceo

Who am I?

I was raised by parents who taught me strong values from an early age. When I was four years old my father taught me Golden Rule - “Do unto others as you would have them do unto you”. My mother taught me to be thoughtful and considerate of others. Both taught me listening skills, empathy, the value of hard work, dedication to family and individual responsibility. In both my personal and business life my parents teachings have been continuously validated and reinforced. My biggest and most gratifying reward is the personal satisfaction of knowing that I have genuinely served others.

Experience

Steve’s decades of experience teaching in the workplace, knowledge of employee benefits, communication skills and “keep your own money approach” make him uniquely prepared to help employees understand, appreciate and value their employer sponsored benefits.!

Financial Literacy

Teaching Financial Literacy since 2003, and serving on the Board for the Financial Literacy Coalition of Central Texas, Steve is more than capable to help you in your financial needs.

Communicator

Steve is a natural communicator and storyteller who specializes in translating complex subjects into simple, everyday language that can be understood by the average person. Over several decades he has effectively delivered training to thousands of people.

Value

Utilizing a holistic fashion, Steve adds value to any project you undertake with him.

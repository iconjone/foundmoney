Who am I?

I was raised by parents who taught me strong values from an early age. When I was four years old my father began explaining the Golden Rule “Do unto others as you would have them do unto you” and the Marine Corps Motto “Semper Fidelis” - always faithful. My mother taught me to cook, clean a house, and be considerate of others. Both parents taught me empathy, listening skills, the value of hard work, dedication to family and individual responsibility. These values learned from my parents have proven to be priceless and for that I am eternally grateful. Having employed these values in my personal and business life, the teachings of my parents have been validated and continually reinforced. My biggest and most gratifying reward is the personal satisfaction of knowing that I have genuinely helped others with creative, custom tailored plans and solutions.
